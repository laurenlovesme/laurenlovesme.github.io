Drafting Techniques

Read below for a brief summary on things to think about when drafting your team.

When drafting your team there is many things to think about such as injuries. Are they known for getting hurt alot? Do they cause trouble on the field? Do they still play with the same team as last season when they did great? for example if the top WR in the league gets traded to a team with a QB that isnt good that player is most likley not gonna have the same season as he did before. Another thing to check for is bye weeks. You do not want to have most of players off the same week because then you have to go looking on the waiver wire for other players to replace them for that week which means you will have to drop players. Remember this game is all luck there is no skill you never know what will happen. Therefore ask yourself the simple questions and dont think twice about it.
